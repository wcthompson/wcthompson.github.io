Based on basically nothing at all

While failing to hail a cab, an unstylish Canadian teenager had an idea: Why not dress as a skyscraper and interrogate people?

While watching happy-slapping videos online, a jobless Thai woman had an idea: Why not dress as a giraffe and pray with people?

While double-parking, an almost illiterate Turkish teenager had an idea: Why not dress as a robot and do good deeds for people?

While stepping over a street pizza, a cockeyed Armenian woman had an idea: Why not dress as a carnivorous plant and soothe people?
