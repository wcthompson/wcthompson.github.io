Hypertexual Starring of The Matrix

About

Barthes methodology of starring a text involves pouring through the symbolic coding of every moment of a text and breaking it down into it's 5 symbolic codes - Hermeneutic (HER), Proairetic (ACT), Semantic (SEM), Symbolic (SYM), and Cultural (REF). This intensely analytic approach is surprisingly similar to the hypertextual annotations New York City startup Genius (formerly 'RapGenius') allows users to leave over the lyrics of songs of all genres, and a varity of other mediums, including classic literature and even television.

In a move to expand beyond the annotation of song lyrics, Genius released a browser extension called the Web Annotator. The annotator adds a mutual, "stereographic" hypertexual space to all text anywhere on the internet. Anyone with the extension can view the annotations of anyway else, and they can include images and gifs and hyperlinks in addition to text. I believe that Genius' is founded on Barthes' principals, making it the perfect tool for my own close-reading. I will be using the genius web annotator extension to star the script from two scenes of The Matrix (script source here). In addition to using Barthes 5 codes, I am introducing my own code - the aesthetic code (AES - to refer to the visual cues in scene that are specific to a visual medium, such as film. In more depth, the aesthetic code deals with cinematography, set and costume design, generally speaking things that may not be in the script itself, but are clear to any viewer.

Spoon Scene

INT. ORACLE'S APARTMENT

It seems particularly normal.

PRIESTESS

Make yourself at home, Morpheus.

MORPHEUS

Thank you.

PRIESTESS

Neo, come with me.

She leads Neo down another hall and into what appears to be a family room.

There is another woman in white sitting on a couch watching a soap opera. Scattered about the room are a half dozen children. Some of them are playing, others are deep in meditation. All of them exude a kind of Zen calm.

PRIESTESS

These are the other Potentials. You can wait here.

Neo watches a little girl levitate wooden alphabet blocks. Closer to him, a SKINNY BOY with a shaved head holds a spoon which sways like a blade of grass. In front of him is a pile of spoons bent and twisted into knots. Neo crosses to him and sits. The boy smiles and hands Neo the spoon which is now perfectly straight.

SPOON BOY (SKINNY BOY)

Do not try to bend the spoon. That is impossible. Instead, only try to realize the truth.

NEO

What truth?

SPOON BOY

That there is no spoon. Neo nods, staring at the spoon.

NEO

There is no spoon.

SPOON BOY

Then you will see that it is not the spoon that bends. It is only yourself.

The entire room is reflected inside the spoon and as Neo stares into it, it slowly begins to bend until -- A hand touches his shoulder.

PRIESTESS

The Oracle will see you now. Spoon Boy smiles.
